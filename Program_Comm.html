Program Committee

Northwest District, Erie Shores Council, BSA

The district activities and civic service committee'’'s job is to provide mountaintop experiences that dramatically capture the attention of the whole Scouting community.

Tasks of the Program Committee include:

  1. Develop and implement a plan for activities and civic service projects in the district.
  2. Ensure that activities remain in accordance with national policy.
  3. Support and strengthen units by assuring program visibility and a well-balanced schedule of activities.
  4. Promote and conduct displays and skill events:
    1. booth shows
    2. camporees
    3. first aid contests
    4. swim meets
    5. window displays
    6. shopping mall shows
  5. Encourage Good Turn ideas through units' participation in community projects and civic service activities.
  6. Plan, promote, and conduct special Scouting Anniversary Week activities.
  7. Implement council activities and civic service program.
  8. Conduct an annual poll of unit leaders to determine unit needs and wishes for district activities.
  9. Oversee the district’s Good Turn for America projects.
