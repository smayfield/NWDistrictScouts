Leadership Committee

Northwest District, Erie Shores Council, BSA
District operations are carried out by Scouting volunteers who serve on the various committees that mobilize resources to ensure the growth and success of units within the district’s territory. These committees handle the four standard functions of the District: membership, finance, program, and unit service.
