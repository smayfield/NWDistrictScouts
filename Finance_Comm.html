Finance Committee

Northwest District, Erie Shores Council, BSA

Each district within the council has finance goals. These goals are based upon a fair-share formula, based on the potential of the district and the needs of the council.

Tasks of the Finance/FOS Committee include:

  1. Ensure implementation of council finance policies.
  2. Achieve the district's share of council finance campaigns.
  3. Organize and carry out a successful Friends of Scouting annual campaign, and meet the goal by the targeted date.
  4. Support the council project selling program.
  5. Support the council endowment development plan.
  6. Inform units of unit financing policy and assist in the review and approval of unit requests.
  7. Support district activities that involve income and expenses, ensuring proper policy and controls.
  8. Provide recognition to donors, along with information on how their dollars helped serve youth.
