Construction City 2013
 Webelos are invited to come have fun as they build Cities and Bridges out of cardboard.
City Division:
This division requires the scouts to build structures on 8’X6’ plot of land with supplied cardboard, tape and tools after completing the permitting process. Division limited to 360 1 st or 2nd year Webelos.
Bridge Division
This division requires the scouts to build a bridge spanning 12’ capable of holding scale weights while controlling structure weight of materials with supplied cardboard, tape and tools. Division limited to 84 2nd year Webelos.
Saturday, November 30, 2013
7:45am - 3:30pm
in the Health Education Center on the University of Toledo Main Campus
