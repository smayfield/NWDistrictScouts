The Latest Training Opportunities

The district training committee's primary job is to get adult leaders trained. Tasks of the Training Committee also include:

  1. Establish district training objectives to train leaders.
  2. Prepare an inventory of all leaders who need training.
  3. Plan, schedule, and coordinate an annual district training program.
  4. Evaluate and report on training progress.
  5. 5. Maintain unit and district training records, and coordinate them with the council registrar
  6. Offer training opportunities to every adult volunteer.
  7. Promote attendance at all training courses to ensure maximum attendance and participation.
  8. Approve applications for district training recognitions.
  9. Track and attain training objectives.

Goals

  1. To make sure that every Scout gets the trained leader that they deserve.
  2. To coordinate training efforts with other Districts in order to increase access to resources (supplies and people) and increase training opportunities for volunteers.
